Duas Collection

Essential daily duas with meaning

Safar ki Dua

Arabic:
Subhanallazi sakhkhara lana haza...

Meaning: Glory be to Allah who has subjected this for us...

Sone ki Dua

Arabic:
Allahumma bismika amutu wa ahya

Meaning: O Allah, in Your name I die and I live

Uthne ki Dua

Arabic:
Alhamdulillahillazi ahyana...

Meaning: All praise is for Allah who gave us life after death

Khana khane ki Dua

Arabic:
Bismillah

Meaning: In the name of Allah
