About DeenGuidance

DeenGuidance is a platform dedicated to providing authentic Islamic knowledge and resources.

Our mission is to help Muslims learn, practice, and share Islam through:

  • Duas (Supplications)
  • Hadith (Teachings of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ)
  • Zikr (Remembrance of Allah)
  • Islamic Video Scripts
  • Free Islamic Resources

We aim to make Islamic knowledge accessible to everyone in a simple and practical way.
